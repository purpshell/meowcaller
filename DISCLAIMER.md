## Purpose

meowcaller is an independent **research and interoperability** project. It documents a
model of the WhatsApp 1:1 call protocol in GO so that the community can understand WhatsApp calls. 
It exists to create knowledge, not to disrupt or attack any service or user.

## Ground rules for research and data

To keep this project ethical and safe, all contributions must follow these rules:

- **Only your own accounts and devices.** Conduct research solely with accounts and
  devices you own or for which you have explicit, informed consent from the other
  party in a test call. Never observe, intercept, or analyze another person's
  communications.
- **No real user data, ever.** Do not commit real phone numbers, JIDs, contact
  information, cryptographic keys, tokens, or call media. Every example in this
  repository must be **synthetic or fully sanitized** and labelled as such.
- **No secrets in the spec.** Document the *structure and behavior* of the protocol,
  not recovered secret values. Describe how something works abstractly; do not paste
  key material.
- **Respect people.** This work must not be used to track, deanonymize, or harm anyone.

If you spot data that violates these rules, report it per the
[security policy](./SECURITY.md) so it can be removed.
